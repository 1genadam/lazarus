Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our walk-in tubs.

Our 21" wide seats accommodate most users comfortably. Measure your relaxed hip and shoulder width - the seat should be larger than these measurements. Use our sizing calculator above or schedule a consultation for personalized recommendations.

Professional installation typically takes 1-2 days with minimal disruption to your daily routine. Our licensed electricians and plumbers handle all technical requirements including electrical work and plumbing connections.

We recommend a 75+ gallon water heater for optimal performance. Our consultation includes professional assessment of your current water heater capacity. The built-in 650W inline heater helps maintain temperature during therapy sessions.

Our proprietary dual drain technology empties the tub in approximately 80 seconds under ideal conditions. The gravity-driven system with four 2" drain ports provides reliable, fast drainage without electrical components that could fail.

Absolutely. Our tubs feature low 4-inch threshold entry, ADA-compliant grab bars, non-slip textured flooring, and internal door locking. The 17" seat height and 21" width accommodate wheelchair transfers and mobility aid users.

Monthly deep cleaning with water and white vinegar (run systems for 10-15 minutes). Daily maintenance is not required. The ozone sterilization and auto-purge systems keep the tub clean automatically. Use non-abrasive cleaners and avoid bath bombs.
