About Us

Learn more about our history, mission, and values.

Our Story

Lazarus Home Remodeling was founded in 2005 by Mark Lazarus with a vision to transform houses into dream homes. Starting as a small team, we've grown into a leading remodeling company known for quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service. Our journey is marked by countless successful projects and satisfied homeowners.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide homeowners with innovative and personalized remodeling solutions that enhance their living spaces and reflect their unique styles. We are committed to delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality.

Our Values

Quality

We use only the finest materials and employ skilled craftsmen to ensure lasting results.

Customer Satisfaction

We prioritize our clients' needs and strive to exceed their expectations at every step.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and a commitment to ethical practices.

Meet Our Team

Robert Sher (Founder & CEO) and Rachel Sher (CMO)

Robert and Rachel Sher are the owners of Lazarus Home Remodeling, bringing together a shared passion for transforming homes and enhancing lives. Together, they manage a team of highly skilled remodelers, craftsmen, artisans, and carpenters who have decades of combined experience making their customers' vision come to life. Their commitment to quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service has made Lazarus Home Remodeling a trusted name in home renovation, where every project is approached with meticulous attention to detail and a dedication to exceeding expectations.
